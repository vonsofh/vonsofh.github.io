## Welcome!

This is my first blog post on my new portfolio site. I'm excited to start sharing my thoughts, learnings, and experiences in the world of web development.

## What to Expect

I plan to write about:

- **Technical tutorials** — step-by-step guides on the tools and frameworks I use
- **Project breakdowns** — behind-the-scenes of the projects I build
- **Lessons learned** — things I wish I knew sooner
- **Industry insights** — thoughts on trends in web development

## Why Blog?

Writing helps me solidify my understanding of various topics and give back to the community that has taught me so much. If even one post helps someone solve a problem faster, it's worth it.
