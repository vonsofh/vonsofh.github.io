## About the Project

DISCOVER BATAM is a company profile website and digital travel guide for Batam City. It features a directory of tourist destinations, food recommendations, hotels, and travel information for both local and international visitors.

### Key Features

- **Tourism directory** — Comprehensive list of Batam attractions with descriptions and locations
- **Dining recommendations** — Restaurant and local food guide for Batam
- **Hotel listings** — Accommodation options in Batam with details and contact info
- **Travel information** — Transportation, visa, and tips for visiting Batam
- **SEO-friendly** — Optimized for search engines to help tourists find the site

### Architecture

Built with Laravel as the CMS and backend. Content is managed through an admin panel and served through a responsive, fast-loading front end. On-page SEO optimization applied throughout to maximize search engine visibility.
