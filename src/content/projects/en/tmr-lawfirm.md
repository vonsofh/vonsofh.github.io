## About the Project

TMR LAWFIRM is a company profile website for a law firm. Features multi-language CMS, lawyer profiles, practice area descriptions, and a case inquiry system.

### Key Features

- **Multi-language CMS** — Content management with language switching
- **Lawyer profiles** — Individual attorney profiles with expertise areas
- **Practice areas** — Detailed service descriptions by legal specialization
- **Case inquiry** — Client consultation request form
- **Admin panel** — Full content management for all website sections

### Architecture

Built with Laravel featuring multi-language content management. Admin panel allows lawyers to manage their profiles, practice areas, and respond to inquiries.
