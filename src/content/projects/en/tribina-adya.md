## About the Project

TRIBINA ADYA LOGISTIK is a logistics company website featuring multi-language support and package tracking functionality. Showcases freight forwarding services with an integrated tracking system.

### Key Features

- **Multi-language** — Indonesian and English language support
- **Package tracking** — Real-time shipment tracking by AWB number
- **Service catalog** — Comprehensive freight forwarding service listings
- **Admin CMS** — Content management for pages, services, and company info
- **Contact & inquiry** — Service inquiry form with email notifications

### Architecture

Laravel handles the CMS backend and tracking system. Multi-language implementation with locale-based routing. Tracking integration provides real-time shipment status updates.
