## About the Project

SUPERSISTEM is a guest reservation system for managing visitor bookings, meeting room scheduling, and guest coordination. Features automated email notifications for booking confirmations.

### Key Features

- **Guest booking** — Online reservation form for visitors
- **Meeting scheduling** — Room and meeting slot management
- **Email notifications** — Automated confirmation and reminder emails
- **Visitor tracking** — Guest check-in/check-out logging
- **Admin dashboard** — Overview of upcoming reservations and statistics

### Architecture

Laravel handles the reservation engine and email notification system. MySQL stores booking data with conflict detection. Email queue ensures reliable notification delivery.
