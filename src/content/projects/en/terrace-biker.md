## About the Project

TERRACE BIKER is an inventory management system for a bike parts shop. Tracks item inflow and outflow with detailed logging and stock reporting.

### Key Features

- **Stock in/out** — Record incoming and outgoing inventory with timestamps
- **Movement log** — Full audit trail of all stock movements
- **Stock reports** — Current stock levels and movement summaries
- **Item management** — Product catalog with categories and pricing
- **Low-stock alerts** — Notifications when items fall below thresholds

### Architecture

Built with Laravel and MySQL. Stock movement engine records every transaction with full traceability. Bootstrap-based admin interface for inventory management.
