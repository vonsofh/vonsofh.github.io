## About the Project

MEMBER AREA INUL VIZTA is a member loyalty platform for Inul Vizta Batam (karaoke & entertainment venue). Members accumulate points from visits and transactions, then redeem them for rewards.

### Key Features

- **Member registration** — Customer signup with profile management
- **Point accumulation** — Automatic points earned from transactions
- **Point redemption** — Redeem points for rewards and discounts
- **Transaction history** — Complete visit and spending history
- **Admin management** — Member data, point adjustments, and reward configuration

### Architecture

Laravel application with member authentication and point calculation engine. Points are automatically credited based on transaction amounts and redeemed through the member portal.
