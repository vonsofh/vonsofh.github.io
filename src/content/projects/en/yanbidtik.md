## About the Project

YANBIDTIK is a member management system for POLDA KEPRI (Kepulauan Riau Provincial Police). Manages personnel data, internal ticketing, and generates organizational reports.

### Key Features

- **Personnel database** — Complete member profiles and service records
- **Ticketing system** — Internal request and issue tracking
- **Organizational reports** — Hierarchical reporting and statistics
- **Role-based access** — Multi-level permissions matching police hierarchy
- **Document management** — Digital storage of member certificates and documents

### Architecture

Laravel application with role-based access control mirroring the organizational hierarchy. MySQL database stores personnel records with historical tracking. Internal ticketing for operational requests.
